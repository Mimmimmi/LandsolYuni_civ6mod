[h1]优妮、可可萝（公主）、镜华领导的兰德索尔：先睹为快[/h1]

优妮、可可萝、镜华位列《公主连结 Re:Dive》最受欢迎的角色之中。本领袖包包含了三位体型娇小的领袖，在儿童节到来之际为《文明6》添加一抹童趣。

兰德索尔的特色建筑是公会之家，可为城市提供额外的生产力，并为每个领袖提供独特的加成。特色单位“王宫骑士团炮”解锁于文艺复兴时期，拥有更强的战斗力，且不需要战略资源。

优妮的特色能力是“博学者的教诲”。建设图书馆可以获得额外的大作家点数和文学著作槽位，且文学著作可以提供额外的科技值。她同样带来了“优妮旋翼机”单位，比其所替代的无人机要更早解锁且更为廉价。

文明特性“兰德索尔公主骑士”是一种特别的能力，对于每位领导兰德索尔的领袖而言，它都提供截然不同的加成。对于优妮，此特性提供的是“认知发展哲学”，令学院、剧院广场和战略资源上的矿山同时提供科技值和文化值，且单位在生产或升级完成后能获得一次免费的晋升。

可可萝的“公主”造型的独特能力是“纯白神启”。国家公园可以为城市提供产出加成。若与山脉、森林或自然奇观相邻，单元格产出额外信仰。公主可可萝还提供“爱梅斯巫女”单位，这一平民单位在王权神授市政即可解锁，可以建设国家公园或者植树造林。

“兰德索尔公主骑士”特性为她提供的是“极光圣界”。建设保护区的速度加快，可以从中获得额外的文化、生产力甚至食物，保护区建筑的效果范围也增大了一个单元格。此外，在魅力较高的单元格，单位可以获得额外回复。（若您没有越南DLC，她的能力会有所改变。详情请查看游戏内描述。）

镜华的独特能力是“小小优等生”。拥有学院区域的城市获得额外科技值和生产力。若一座城市的3个单元格范围内有5个海岸单元格，且这些单元格为她所拥有，则这些产出加倍。

“兰德索尔公主骑士”特性为她提供的是“宇宙蓝闪光”，令水域可以为他们提供大量战术和战略优势。学院可以从河流获得额外的大量相邻加成，从海岸获得标准的相邻加成。沿河或沿海的陆地远程单位获得额外射程，单元格则可以拥有额外的生产力。一旦他们建成了一座宇航中心，便可以立即解锁2项对科技胜利至关重要的免费科技。

您能够成为小小的伟大领袖吗？您将会如何带领兰德索尔建立经得起时间考验的文明？

[h1]模组特性：[/h1]

[b]强力而不超模。[/b]每位领袖的能力均鼓励一种独特的游玩方式，并在设计时充分考虑了领袖的能力强度，使其拥有充分的竞争力但并不显著过强。

[b]忠于原作。[/b]本作品在制作过程中进行了精细的考证，包括人名、地名、城市名称和外交用语，以尽最大可能表现原作的风土人情和领袖性格特征。

[b]和谐风格。[/b]所有已制作的美术素材均参照《文明6》游戏的规格进行处理，确保其在各个界面正确显示且整洁清爽。

[b]背景音乐和领袖语音（仅优妮）。[/b]是谁从Wwise Soundbank中寻找了播放音频的正确方式？是的，就是我！

[h1]已知问题：[/h1]

文学著作提供的额外科技值可能与其他模组冲突，致使其效果无法正常工作。

音乐播放有时会存在问题，具体表现与官方的越南和葡萄牙文明相同。

文明百科仅支持简体中文。

爱梅斯巫女的宗教传播次数或创建国家公园次数的任意一个耗尽均会导致该单位被解散。

可可萝和镜华的议程在关系面板中会显示为“未知原因”，但效果正常生效。

若开启“栩栩如生的领袖”，有模型的领袖的动画播放可能会出现问题。

本模组替换了加载界面和外交界面以隐藏不需要的“兰德索尔公主骑士”字样。若您安装有修改以上界面的模组，您将会看到本条特性，但没有更多效果。

[h1]Yuni, Kokkoro (Princess) and Kyoka Lead Landosol: First Look[/h1]

Yuni, Kokkoro and Kyoka are some of the most popular characters in the mobile game Princess Connect Re:Dive. This mod contains three little leaders, and brings some cuteness to Civilization VI to celebrate China's Children's Day (June 1).

Guildhouse is the unique building of Landosol, which provides Production and unique bonuses for each Landsol leader. The unique unit Royal Palace Cannon is unlocked at Renaissance Era, with stronger Combat Strength and don't need any Strategical resource.

Yuni's unique ability is Knowledge of Erudites. Constructing Library can provide extra Great Writer Points and Writing great work slot, and Writing great works provide extra Science. She also unlocks Yunicopter, which is available earlier and cheaper than the Drone unit it replaces. 

The unique ability of Landosol civilization Princess Knight is a special ability that provides entirely different abilities to each Landosol leader. For Yuni, this ability grants Philosophy of Cognition and Development, which allows Campuses, Theater Squares and Mines on strategic resources provide Science and Culture at the same time, and let units receive a free Promotion when they are produced or upgraded.

The Princess Persona of Kokkoro has an unique ability named Pure White Revelation. Cities with a National Park receive bonus yields. Tiles provide additional Faith when adjacent to Mountains, Woods, or Natural Wonders. Princess Kokkoro also provides the Miko of Ameth unique unit. This unique civilian unit is unlocked with Divine Right, and can creating a National Park or plant Woods. 

Princess Knight grants her Aurora Sanctuary. Kokkoro can build Preserves faster, receive bonus Culture, Production or even Food from them, and the effect range of Preserve buildings are extended by 1. Also, Units heal faster in tiles with high Appeal. (Her ability changes if you don't have Vietnam DLC. For details check in-game description.)

The unique ability of Kyoka is Little Model Student. Cities with a Campus district will have increased output of Science and Production. If there are 5 Coast tiles within 3 tiles of a city and owned by her, these yields are doubled.

Princess Knight grants her Cosmo Blue Flash, allowing water to provide them with tactical and strategic advantages. Campuses are granted an extra Major adjacency with Rivers and a Standard adjacency from Coasts. When adjacent to a River or Coast, Ranged units receive Range bonus, and tiles can have additional Production. Additionally, Once they have constructed a Spaceport, they can instantly unlock 2 free technologies vital for a Science Victory.

Can you become a little great leader? How will you lead Landosol to build a civilization that stands the test of time?

[h1]Mod Highlights:[/h1]

[b]"Powered" without "Over"[/b]: Each Landosol leader has several abilities that promote an unique play-style. This mod is designed to make abilities of all leaders included competent but not overpowered.

[b]Lore-Loyalty[/b]: This mod is made with a fine research of the original Princess Connect franchise, and provides full lists of named places, citizen names, city names and diplomacy statements to better represent lore and leader characteristics.

[b]Harmonized Mix[/b]: All art assets included in this mod are processed to fit the need of Civilization VI, so that they can display correctly and neatly.

[b]Background Music and Leader Speech (Yuni only)[/b]: Who found the correct way to play audios in Wwise Soundbanks? Yes, that was me!

[h1]Known issues:[/h1]

Extra Science from Writing great works will conflict with other mods that also provide the same bonus, thus causing Science output of Writing great works to be calculated erroneously.

Music playback sometimes can be glitchy, with the same issues of official Vietnam and Portugal civilizations.

Civilopedia article is in Simplified Chinese only.

The Miko of Ameth unit will be disbanded once either Religion Spread Charges or National Park Charge is depleted.

Kokkoro and Kyoka's Agendas will be shown as Unknown Reason in relationship panel, but their effects works correctly.

Animations of leaders with a 3D model might have minor playback issue if Animated Leader is enabled.

This mod replaces loading screen and diplomacy screen to hide unneeded Princess Knight entries (as it becomes an unique ability of each leader). If you enabled mods that replace them, you will see a redundant unique ability without extra effects.
